- **CONSENT**: It is the free, informed, and unequivocal manifestation by which the USER agrees to the rules set forth in this statement, including the use of their personal or anonymized data;

- **CONTENT**: Any information, data, communications, software, photos, videos, graphics, music, sounds, and other materials and services that can be viewed by the USER on the PLATFORM. This includes messages, conversations, chat, and other original content.

- **CONTROLLER**: legal entity responsible for decisions regarding the processing of personal data.

- **SHARING**: refers to the sharing of USER data, including Personal Data, and all CONTENT associated with third parties.

- **COOKIES**: files sent by the SITE server to the USER's computer, tablet, cell phone, or other devices, in order to identify and obtain access data, such as pages browsed, browser type, searches performed, and links clicked, allowing personalization of SITE usage according to your profile. They can also be used to ensure greater USER security on the SITE.

- **PERSONAL DATA**: all data related to a natural person (identified or identifiable), relating to their identity and private life.

- **IP (Internet Protocol)**: is a set of numbers that identifies the USER's computer, tablet, cell phone, or other devices on the Internet.

- **LOGS**: records of USER activities performed on the SITE.

- **DATA MESSAGES**: information generated, sent, received, stored, or communicated by electronic, optical, or similar means.

- **OPERATOR**: natural or legal person, public or private, who processes personal data on behalf of the Controller.

- **SERVICES**: services offered through the SITE.

- **PROCESSING**: any operation or set of operations performed on personal data or on sets of personal data, whether by automated means or not.

- **USER**: natural and legal persons registered on the SITE.

HRecruiter and the USER may be mentioned individually in this Instrument as "Party" or together as "Parties".

4.2 The data and information collected will be encrypted during traffic and stored in a secure environment, observing the state of the art available.

4.4 We use the USER's PERSONAL DATA for as long as necessary to achieve the purposes described herein, as well as to comply with applicable legal and contractual obligations.

4.5 To determine the retention period of PERSONAL DATA, we use the following criteria:

- For compliance with legal or contractual obligations, PERSONAL DATA will be kept for a period of 5 (five) years;
- For commercial and financial purposes, they will be kept for a period of 5 (five) years;

4.6 We will preserve your Personal Data in accordance with the security and confidentiality procedures indicated in applicable law and regulations.
